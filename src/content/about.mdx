I am a maker of cool things for the web.  I like to create small digital experiments for myself, and this is my digital home.  Here I will document what I build, and hopefully make it become something useful for other people.

The internet made me less lonely when I was a kid, and I hope to do the same for other people.  I would like to see the web become more open, more weird, and that living it becomes a journey for people to find wonder in life.

The loneliness before the screen binds us together, and it doesn't have to be an ugly road.  The web is beautiful, sharing is good, and it can help us find purpose and joy.
